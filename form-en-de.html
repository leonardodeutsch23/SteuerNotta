Prüfungen vor dem Absenden · Checks before submission

This review helps verify the completeness of the case file, but it does not certify the tax return or replace a professional tax review.

  • The reference year is 2025 and the data correspond to that fiscal period.
  • All relevant persons, employers and wage tax certificates are included.
  • Net amounts and reimbursements have been indicated separately to avoid duplication.
  • Signals outside the current scope have been identified for reviewing their official annexes.
